Helping Hand

Challenging the notion of traditional volunteering.

Helping Hand

Helping hand allows users to connect with like-minded individuals who wish to make a social impact. Here, they can receive task offers from various volunteering organizations similar to other crowd-sourcing programs, essentially allowing users to volunteer remotely.

Explore Opportunities

Users can view all of their in-progress tasks and easily start where they left off. New opportunities are available to all users and new tasks will be recommended based on their interests and experience.

Track Your Impact

Unlike other volunteering positions where the ultimate effect is never communicated to the volunteer, Helping hand provides a portal for organizations to update the volunteer on how they have made an impact.
